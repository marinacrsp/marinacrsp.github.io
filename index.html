About Me

Hey there ! I am a MSc student in Biomedical Engineering at ETH Zürich. Currently completing my MSc thesis at the A. Martinos Center for Biomedical Imaging, supervised by Prof. Iglesias and Prof. Konukoglu. My current work focuses on the implementation of DeepL pipelines for the reconstruction of Synthetic Ex-Vivo MRI using Dissection Photography.

Featured Projects

Neural Implicit Fields for the reconstruction of fastMRI

class="pub-description">Neural Implicit Fields for reconstructing MRI scans from undersampled, aliased data. The dataset used for this project belongs to the open-source fastMRI dataset. The repository contains different training pipelines, regularization losses and input encoding strategies. The research forms part of a Semester Project carried out at CVL (Fall 2024).

Medical Image Generation, Retraining on a Strict Budget

Low-computation approach for Data Augmentation using a Conditioned Latent Diffusion Model to generate synthetic MRI scans. We implemented the formulation proposed in SeLoRA to fine-tune a Text-Conditioned Latent Diffusion Model on two small training datasets (BraTS2021, & PI-CAI). We validated the generated outputs on a downstream classifier, with comparatively better AUC scores than baselines (i.e. GANS, LoRA). This work belongs to an assignment from the course Deep Learning, ETH Zürich (Fall 2024).
